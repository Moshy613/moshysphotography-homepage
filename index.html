About Moshy

Based in the vibrant city of Montreal, I'm a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing the authentic beauty of life's moments. From the bustling streets of Old Montreal to intimate portrait sessions, my work focuses on storytelling through visual artistry.

With years of experience in photography, I specialize in street photography, portraits, urban landscapes, and event coverage. My goal is to freeze time and create images that evoke emotion and preserve memories for a lifetime.
